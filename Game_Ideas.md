Ideas


Burning Building Escape - start on the top floor of a building on fire and race down to escape the fire - hopefully not triggering to people who've been trapped in a burning building

Zombie Escape - Office building infested with zombies - get to the ground floor exit or the roof to escape 

Burglar Mode - You are a security guard in an office building and you get radio alerts that there are burglars in the building.  Find a lift, go to the floor that the burglars are on and catch them before they change floors. You catch a burglar if you get within a certain distance and he's in your line of sight. 

Office Intern Mode - you are an office intern and you receive jobs that require visting different floors, finding the item and delivering to another office on another floor.
